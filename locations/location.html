WATERWAY 5A

Located at the Waterway 5A in New Cairo, just off the Ring Road and 90th Street. Minimal design and clean lines define this store. Enjoy a coffee inside or out in the sun.

DARA'S ICE CREAM COFFEE BARS

We’ve opened up espresso bars at our favorite ice cream shop Dara’s Ice Cream. You’ll find our coffee bars at Dara’s Ice Cream branches in Maadi, Zamalek, and O1 Mall in New Cairo. There you can get most of our espresso based coffee drinks, iced and hot, as well as some exclusive milkshakes.

MARASSI MARINA

Our Sahel flagship store located at the entrance of Marassi Marina. Enjoy outdoor and indoor seating area that is designed to make you feel at home while you sip slowly on your favorite cup of coffee.
