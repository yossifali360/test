ROASTED IN THE HEART OF GIZA

Brown Nose Coffee is a specialty coffee roaster in the heart of Cairo, Giza.

We work with leading farms and specialty coffee importers from across the globe to source the top specialty graded coffee green beans with certificates from the American Specialty Coffee Association.

TRAINING

What’s good coffee if not prepared properly? We understand that training your baristas to prepare the perfect cup can be daunting. Let us help you take care of that.

EQUIPMENT

We work with the best equipment, and our experience in the industry allows us to confidently source the proper supplies for your shop.

SUPPORT

We’re here to build a long-term relationship and help you grow and become a community hub. Ongoing checkups and refresher trainings is only a small part of it.
